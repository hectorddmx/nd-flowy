# About WorkFlowy

## What is WorkFlowy?

WorkFlowy is a simple, smooth digital notebook that replaces traditional notebooks, sticky notes, and bloated productivity apps. It provides a minimalist outliner interface built around infinitely nesting bullet points.

## Key Features

- **Infinitely nesting lists** - Organize thoughts hierarchically with unlimited depth
- **Notes with files & images** - Attach media and additional notes to any bullet
- **Boards** - Track activities with kanban-style boards
- **Cross-platform sync** - Access your data from any device
- **Powerful search** - Find anything instantly across all your content
- **Sharing & collaboration** - Share individual bullets or entire documents
